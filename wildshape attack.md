Roll one of your beast form's attacks. In combat it uses the attacks granted by the Wild Shape effect.

__Usage__
`!wildshape attack bite -t goblin`
`!wildshape atk claw -t goblin -t orc`

Pass-through args (`-t`, `adv`, `-d`, and so on) work as normal. Active damage riders (Primal Strike, Lunar
Radiance/Form) are shown as reminders; add their dice with `-d` when you use them.
