Roll an ability check using the **higher** of your beast-form modifier or the beast's stat block. You keep
your skill proficiencies and gain the beast's, all at your Proficiency Bonus. STR/DEX/CON checks use the
beast's physical scores (your INT/WIS/CHA are unchanged), so a physical check can come out higher or lower
than your true form. Works in or out of combat.

__Usage__
`!wildshape check stealth`
`!wildshape check "animal handling" adv`
