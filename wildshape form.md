Transform into a known form. Spends a Wild Shape use and grants Temp HP equal to your Druid level (3x for
Circle of the Moon), keeping the higher amount if you already have temp HP. In combat it overlays the
beast's AC, resistances, attacks, and per-ability save bonuses, with a Revert button.

__Usage__
`!wildshape form Wolf`
`!wildshape Wolf`  (shortcut, no `form` needed)
