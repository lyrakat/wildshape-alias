Step 2 of learning a form. Reads the scanned beast, stores it as a known form, and removes the temporary
combatant.

__Usage__
`!wildshape learn`
`!wildshape learn -as Wolf -cr 1/4`

__Arguments__
`-as Name`: name the form (defaults to the beast's name).
`-cr`, `-fly`, `-size`: set these here, or override what you passed to `scan`.

A form over your Max CR or known-forms limit is still stored, with a note.
