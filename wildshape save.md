Roll a saving throw using the higher of your beast-form modifier or the beast's stat block, plus subclass
bonuses such as Circle of the Moon's Increased Toughness on CON saves. You keep your save proficiencies and
gain the beast's, all at your Proficiency Bonus. STR/DEX/CON saves use the beast's physical scores (your
INT/WIS/CHA are unchanged), so a physical save can come out higher or lower than your true form.

In combat the Wild Shape effect adjusts a plain `!save` directly; this command notices that and won't apply
the bonus a second time, so it's safe to use in or out of combat.

__Usage__
`!wildshape save dex`
`!wildshape save con`
