Step 1 of learning a form. Adds the beast to initiative so its stat block can be read. Requires an active
combat (`!init begin`).

__Usage__
`!wildshape scan wolf -cr 1/4`
`!wildshape scan "giant spider" -cr 1/2 -fly`

__Arguments__
`-cr 1/4`: the beast's Challenge Rating, used for the eligibility check against your level.
`-fly`: the beast has a Fly Speed (usable from level 8).
`-size Medium`: for display.

Then run `!wildshape learn` to store it.
